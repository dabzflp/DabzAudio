Privacy & Cookie Policy

Last updated: 2026

DabzAudio ("we", "us") provides browser-based tools for musicians, producers and DJs. This policy explains what personal data we handle and how we use cookies and similar technologies, following best-practice privacy standards (including the UK GDPR and PECR).

1. What we collect

  • Account data — the email, password (stored only as a secure hash), artist name, unique @username and optional profile picture you provide for the Lyric Book.
  • Your content — lyrics you create and any collaborators you invite.
  • Gift & payout data — when you send or receive a gift, payment is processed by Stripe. We never see or store your full card or bank details; Stripe handles identity checks and payouts.

2. Cookies we use

DabzAudio uses only strictly necessary cookies — the ones required for the site to function. We do not use advertising, marketing or third-party tracking cookies.

CookiePurpose
Session / sign-in Keeps you signed in and secures your session while you use the Lyric Book. The product cannot work without this.
Cookie notice Remembers that you've seen the cookie notice so it doesn't reappear.

Because these cookies are essential, they don't require consent — the notice on the home page is simply to let you know. To clear them, sign out or clear this site's data in your browser.

3. How we use your data

To run your account, store and share your lyrics, process gifts securely via Stripe, and keep the service safe. We do not sell your data and we do not track you across other sites.

4. Sharing

We share data only with service providers that make DabzAudio work — for example Stripe (payments) and our hosting/database provider — and only as needed to deliver the service, or where required by law.

5. Your rights

You can access, correct, export or delete your personal data, and withdraw consent for non-essential cookies at any time. To make a request, contact us below.

6. Contact

Questions or requests: dabzaudio@dabzflp.com.
